DogpatchJS

A weekly free + public hangout for JavaScript enthusiasts that happens in San Francisco, California. No presentations, RSVPs nor startup pitches/overt recruiting allowed. Just show up and talk, learn, and hack!

When

Every Tuesday, 6ish - Late

Where

Special Summer Venue!

Spark Social
601 Mission Bay Boulevard North, San Francisco, CA 94158

Spark Social is family and dog friendly! You are encouraged to bring the whole family!

Smokestack
2505 3rd St, San Francisco, CA 94107

Smokestack is family friendly! You are encouraged to bring your children, significant other etc to DogpatchJS.

All attendees are expected to follow the JSConf Code of Conduct.
