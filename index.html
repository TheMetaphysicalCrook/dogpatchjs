dogpatch.js

A weekly free + public hangout for JavaScript enthusiasts that happens in San Francisco, California. No presentations, RSVPs or startup pitches/overt recruiting allowed, just show up and talk, learn and hack!

When & Where

Every Tuesday, 5:30PM - 8:30PM Smokestack 2505 3rd St, San Francisco, CA 94107

Smokestack is family friendly! You are encouraged to bring your children, significant other etc to DogpatchJS.

This website is open source and you can fork it on GitHub. Site, and the entire idea is shamelessly stoken from OaklandJS.
